Poached Eggs

How to Make Perfect Poached Eggs

Poached eggs are a versatile restaurant delicacy. Enjoy them on toast or english muffins, toss them in a sald, or add them on top of rice or pasta. But you can just as easily make this restaurant breakfast staple at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Put 1 inch of water in a small saucepan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Crack each egg into a shallow bowl and gently slip yolk into water.
  3. Cook 3-5 minutes and lift egg out of pan with a slotted spoon, letting as much water drain off as possible.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy.
