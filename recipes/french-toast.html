French Toast

How to Make Killer French Toast

French toast is a classy and delicious breakfast. It is also really simple to make. With good-quality bread and some basic ingredients, you can have your french toast looking (and tasting) like it came out of a restaurant. The best part is that its a great way to use up slightly stale bread!

Ingredients

  • 8 slices or a half loaf of good-quality bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar, optional
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract, optional
  • 2 tbsp butter, or more

Steps

  1. Put a large skillet over medium-low heat and heat the oven to 200℉.
  2. Whisk eggs and milk in a shallow bowl until mixture is smooth. Then whisk in a small pinch of salt, sugar, and optionally the vanilla extract.
  3. Soak bread slices into the mixture.
  4. Add butter to the skillet and transfer slices to the pan.
  5. Cook each piece until both sides are nicely browned, 3 to 5 minutes each side.
